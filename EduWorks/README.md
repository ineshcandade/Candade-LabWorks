# EduWorks  

---

This folder contains all **concept-focused educational content** developed under **Candade EduWorks**, the educational wing of **Candade LabWorks**.

The material here focuses on explaining electronics and engineering concepts through simple demonstrations and analysis.

Each topic includes its own `README.md` explaining the concept along with links to the corresponding educational videos.

---

## Highlights  
- Clear explanations of electronics concepts  
- Concept demonstrations with worked examples  
- Material focused on explaining engineering topics clearly

---

## Purpose  
To maintain a small archive of concept explanations discussed in the accompanying videos.
